# License Activation Guide

## How to Activate Your License

### Step 1: Open Klip Menu
Click the **ðŸ“‹** icon in your menu bar (top right of screen)

### Step 2: License Activation
When the popover appears:

#### If Unlicensed:
1. You'll see three tabs: **Activate License** | **Try Free** | **About**
2. Click **Activate License** tab
3. Enter your:
   - **Email**: The email you used to purchase
   - **License Key**: Format `CGRD-XXXX-XXXX-XXXX-XXXX`
4. Click **Activate** button

#### Test License (For Development):
- Email: `test@example.com`
- License: `CGRD-TEST-YEAR-2024-DEMO`

### Step 3: After Activation
Once activated, the popover will show:
- **Dashboard** tab - Statistics and activity monitoring
- **Settings** tab - App preferences
- **License** tab - License information and management

## Troubleshooting

### License Key Not Working?
1. Check format: `CGRD-XXXX-XXXX-XXXX-XXXX`
2. Verify email matches purchase email
3. Ensure no extra spaces
4. Try the test license first

### Can't Find Menu Bar Icon?
- Look for **ðŸ“‹** emoji in top-right menu bar
- May be hidden if you have many menu bar items
- Try closing other menu bar apps

### Popover Not Opening?
1. Click directly on the ðŸ“‹ icon
2. Make sure app is running (no dock icon - it's menu bar only)
3. Restart the app if needed
